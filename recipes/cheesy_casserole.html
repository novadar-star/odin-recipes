Corn Sautee

Description

This zucchini casserole with bread cubes, onion, and Cheddar is simple and easy to make for a deliciously cheesy vegetable side dish.

Ingredients

  • 4 slices bread, cubed
  • 2 cups cubed zucchini
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Place bread cubes in a medium bowl and pour melted butter over top. Add zucchini, onion, egg, and garlic salt; mix well.
  3. Cover and bake in the preheated oven for 30 minutes. Uncover the dish and bake for another 30 minutes.
